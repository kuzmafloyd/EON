THE STORY OF A SIMPLE NOMAD

A entrepreneur, a traveller, an amateur photography - with a love for adventure in this big wide world

Photography has always been an interest in my life, starting when I was a toddler and would grab my father’s camera, emptying his entire roll of film aimlessly in a few short minutes. But it wasn’t until I began to travel the world that my love for capturing a fraction of a second, something breathtakingly beautiful or a little crazy became an obsession. I would never classify myself as a "photographer” but more as someone who simply wants to capture the beauty of the ever-changing world for others to see.

The first time I solo-traveled was at the ripe age of 12, crossing the Australian border to visit a friend on the island nation of Fiji. With my disposable camera in hand, I snapped as many photos as I possibly could. It was a gigantic adventure and sparked my desire to travel as much as my life, and piggy bank, would allow. And, I haven’t let go of my camera since.

My travels have brought me to 22 countries, introduced me to amazing people and even inspired me to co-found an international business (The Instutute of Code), based in the beautiful country of Bali. You will inevitably see a lot of photos from this incredible island as I explore more of it and begin to call it my second home.
