THE STORY OF A SIMPLE NOMAD

A entrepreneur, a traveller, a photographer - with a love for adventure in this big wide world

Photography has always been an passion in my life, starting when I was a toddler and would grab my father’s camera, emptying his entire roll of film aimlessly in a few short minutes. But it wasn’t until I began to travel the world that my love for capturing a fraction of a second, something breathtakingly beautiful or a little crazy became an obsession. I would never classify myself as a "photographer” but more as someone who simply wants to capture the beauty of the ever-changing world for others to see.
